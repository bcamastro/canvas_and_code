Women’s lives are always changing...



Every decade brings its own mix of challenges, shifting priorities, and unexpected turns. My mission is to be in your corner through it all including the highs, the lows, and everything in between. I offer a non-judgmental space where you can share the thoughts and feelings that are hard to say out loud and feel safe being fully yourself. Together, we’ll navigate the changes, work through the challenges, and move toward the life you want to be living.

"Every woman deserves support through life’s most complex transitions."
– Jodi Trager Therapy

I’m Jodi!

I’m a licensed clinical social worker and psychotherapist with over 20 years of experience supporting people through life’s many stages. My passion for working with women grew from my own journey through motherhood, when I experienced firsthand how deeply emotional, physical, and relational changes can shape mental health. Those personal experiences gave me a unique perspective on what it means to care for yourself while navigating family, career, relationships, and evolving life roles. I believe every woman deserves to feel seen and understood. My practice is grounded in compassion, inclusivity, and respect, welcoming women and individuals of all backgrounds and identities. It’s an honor to walk alongside women as they care for their mental health, embrace change, and grow into the best version of themselves.

"You’ve survived 100 percent of your worst days" – Robin Arzón

How can therapy help me?

  • Offer a space to slow down, tune in, and feel heard without judgment

  • Help you make sense of what you’re feeling, especially when it’s hard to name

  • Support you through life transitions, planned or unexpected

  • Provide tools for managing anxiety, low mood, and emotional overwhelm

  • Explore patterns in relationships and how they impact your well-being

  • Build confidence in your decisions, boundaries, and voice

  • Untangle the pressure to do it all perfectly or meet everyone’s expectations

  • Help you reconnect with yourself not just as a parent, partner, or professional, but as a person

  • Create more space for your needs without guilt

  • Support you in navigating loss, grief, and other big emotions with care

  • Offer accountability, clarity, and support in showing up for yourself

  • Remind you that you don’t have to hold everything alone
