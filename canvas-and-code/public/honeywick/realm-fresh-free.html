The Realm of Fresh & Free

The Realm of Fresh & Free unfolds into gentle clarity – where the air feels lighter and possibility greets the day.

A soft breeze moves through the space as lemon sparkles, lavender calms the senses, and lemongrass dances lightly in the air. Cool eucalyptus and peppermint trail softly through, clearing the atmosphere.

With Fawndira’s presence carried on the breeze, ease and renewal drift in – a quiet energy that feels bright, hopeful, and free.

An invitation to clear the air, follow the breeze, and dance lightly through the day, where moments twinkle softly.

Lavendergrass Awakening

Scent: Lavender & Lemongrass

Lavendergrass Awakening glows with a soft, citrus-kissed freshness – a blend that lifts the spirit the moment it begins to warm. Bright lemongrass brings a lively spark, lemon adds a gentle shimmer, and lavender settles everything into a calm, balanced ease.

The scent feels clean, airy, and quietly renewing – creating a space that feels open, bright, and naturally refreshed. A beautiful choice for moments when you want your mind clear, your home refreshed, and your mood naturally lifted.

Zesty Morning Mint
