Once Upon a Wick

The Heart of The Honey Wick – Montana Rayne’s Signature Realm

It’s a gentle place of becoming, where intention settles softly and scents move like little stories of their own. Sometimes it’s a warm brush of gingerbread passing through the air. Other times, the hush of cashmere settles close. On certain days, cranberry flickers bright; on others, the cool breath of morning dew drifts in like a fresh beginning.

Each note arrives on its own time – quiet, tender, meaningful – here, story and feeling meet. Each candle is shaped with care, inspired by the chapters that shape us: the dreams we keep reaching toward, and the soft hope of finding ourselves again and again.

Once Upon a Wick is a pre-order, fully customizable realm poured slowly and intentionally – a place for those ready to create something deeply their own. All stories glow to life from a single choice… and yours begins the moment you choose your candle. What are you waiting for?

Montana Rayne’s Personal Favorites

Enchanted Essence

Scent: Gingerbread & Cashmere

When the flame of Enchanted Essence is born, the room shifts into something softer. A warm gingerbread sweetness rises through the air, comforting and inviting, wrapping the space in a hush that feels almost otherworldly. A little cranberry-sparkle glimmers through the blend, bright and playful, while moonlit vanilla glow rests beneath it all – creamy, steady, and glowing.

This is the candle you light when you want cozy evenings to linger, pages to turn a little slower, and the story of your day to end on a note of warmth and wonder.

Only for a Moment

Scent: Bergamot & Cedarwood

There are moments the world grows quiet – when slow, warm early-summer breaths drift through open windows and everything feels suspended between one heartbeat and the next. Only for a Moment is poured for those in-between times.

Dew-soft bergamot, gentle citrus, and a breeze of cedarwood gather into a grounded, sunlit calm. It’s the kind of scent that asks you to stay just a little longer, sip a little slower, breathe a little deeper – and remember that even the smallest moments are worth keeping.
