The Honey Wick — Realmkeeper’s Customer Contract

Safety of Montana Rayne, The Realmkeeper.
In the Land of The Honey Wick, every candle begins as a whisper, a story, a glow waiting to come home. To keep our exchange warm, kind, and full of good-hearted magic, these are the terms every Realmholder agrees to when ordering through the realms.

  1. Who You Are Doing Business With Realm Name: The Honey Wick
    Realmkeeper & Realmdreamer: Montana Rayne
    Enchanted Email: thehoneywickglow@gmail.com
    Home Realm: Inverness, Florida
    Communication Hours: Monday–Friday, 10 am – 5 pm EST. Messages sent outside these hours will be answered the next business day – with care and candlelight.
  2. Crafting & Creation Time Every Honey Wick candle is hand-poured in small, intentional batches using natural waxes and Storyborne essential oils.
    Standard orders: 5–7 business days.
    Custom / Once Upon Your Wick creations: 7–14 business days.
    If a realm is particularly busy, the Realmkeeper will send word.
  3. Shipping Through the Realms Candles travel through trusted carriers with tracking provided. Typical travel time is 3–5 business days. Delays caused by weather, carrier issues, or incorrect addresses are beyond realm magic, but if your package seems lost, email the Realmkeeper – she will help guide it home.
  4. Returns, Exchanges & Replacements Because every candle is handcrafted with intention, all sales are final. However, if a candle arrives damaged or defective, notify the Realmkeeper within 72 hours of delivery with photos of the issue. Once approved, a replacement will be sent at no cost to you. The Honey Wick believes in standing behind every glow.
  5. Glow With Care – Candle Safety Snip the wick ¼ inch before each flicker. Never leave your glow unattended. Keep away from curious hands, flowing fabrics, and heat-sensitive surfaces. Let the story flicker no more than 4 hours at a time.
  6. Allergy & Sensitivity Notes All candles use natural waxes and essential oils. If you are sensitive to botanicals, plant essences, or fragrance, please review scent notes or write to the Realmkeeper for guidance before ordering.
  7. Custom Orders & Once Upon Your Wick Custom and story-crafted orders require a 50% non-refundable deposit to begin, with the remaining balance due before shipping. Once materials are purchased or your story-scent has begun its creation, no cancellations may be made. Custom magic cannot be undone once it begins to glow.
  8. Lore, Artwork & Realm Rights All characters, stories, candle realms, artwork, and lore belong to The Honey Wick and Montana Rayne, the Realmkeeper. They may not be copied, resold, or used in other creations without written permission.
