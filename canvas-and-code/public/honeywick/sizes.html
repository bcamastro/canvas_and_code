The Honey Wick Candle Sizes & Pricing

Every candle from The Honey Wick begins as a whisper from the realms, hand-poured by the Realmdreamer in two enchanted sizes crafted for different kinds of glow.

Each realm unveils two unique Storyborn scents, available as either a Petite Honey Pot or a Dreamer’s Glow Jar.

The Petite Honey Pot – 4 oz – $14

A dainty jar of wanderlight – perfect for sampling a realm, gifting a little glow, or carrying a pocket-sized piece of magic wherever you go.

The Dreamer’s Glow Jar – 7 oz – $22

A radiant, immersive candle crafted for deeper dreaming, warm room-filling moments, and story-soaked evenings.

Wick or No Wick – Your Choice

All Honey Wick candles can be made with a crackling wooden wick, or poured wickless for use on a candle warmer – perfect for those who prefer a flame-free glow.
