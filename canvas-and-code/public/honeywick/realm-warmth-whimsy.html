The Realm of Warmth & Whimsy

The Realm of Warmth & Whimsy glows with gentle comfort – where sweetness lingers and warmth drifts softly through the air.

Sweet orange brightens the space like a small sunbeam, cinnamon hums with cozy spice, and pecan and vanilla melt into a golden softness. Sandalwood and copaiba settle in with a comforting calm, while benzoin adds a creamy, honeyed glow.

With Winnora’s warmth in every flame, ease and comfort gather gently, filling your home with a soft glowing calm.

An invitation to linger a little longer, wrap yourself in warmth, and let comfort glow all around you.

Home Sweet Spice

Scent: Cinnamon & Orange

Home Sweet Spice flickers to life like a comforting spell – cinnamon swirling in soft, golden ribbons while bright orange sparkles through the air like tiny firelit stars. Cozy spices drift in with inviting sweetness, and a cozy pecan-spice glow settles beneath it all as creamy benzoin deepens the blend into a melted-honey hush.

The scent feels alive, soothing, and deeply comforting – the kind of warmth that wraps around you like a familiar embrace. Perfect for creating that magical home-soft glow where everything feels safe, sweet, and wonderfully cozy.

Velvet Dream

Scent: Vanilla & Sandalwood

Indulge in the comforting warmth of Velvet Dream, where creamy vanilla sweetness meets the gentle, grounding richness of sandalwood. To create a softness that feels almost spellborn, this dreamy blend turns any room into a small sanctuary.

A velvety warmth drifts through the space like a little fairytale – sweet, soothing, and wrapped in a hush of wonder. Woven within the glow is a curl of cloud-soft comfort, adding a tender, whimsical warmth that makes the scent feel even more serene and storybook-soft – like being gathered into an embrace you never want to leave.
